PHONESPACE

A storage cleaner that respects your privacy.

Everything runs on your iPhone. Nothing is uploaded. No tracking, no accounts, no compromise.

Built differently

Most cleaner apps want your data. They scan your library in the cloud, sell your usage patterns to advertisers, and bury you under three-day trials that auto-bill weekly.

PhoneSpace is the opposite. One app, one job, done locally on your device.

Privacy, for real

On-device only

Photos, videos, and contacts never leave your iPhone. There's no server to send them to.

No data collection

Zero analytics, tracking SDKs, ad networks, or crash reporters phoning home.

No accounts

No signup, no email required, no password to manage. Download, open, use.

Apple verified

App Store privacy label confirms: "Data Not Collected." It's the truth, not marketing.

What it does

  • Photos Find duplicates, similar photos, screenshots, large files, blurry shots, and bursts.
  • Videos Surface large videos. Compress them to reclaim gigabytes without obvious quality loss.
  • Contacts Detect duplicates and incomplete entries. Smart Merge with review-before-apply. Back up to .vcf.
  • Files Browse, organize, and clean your local files with Quick Look previews built in.
  • Compress Reduce photo and video sizes with three quality presets. See savings before you commit.
  • Convert HEIC ↔ JPG/PNG, video format conversion, and Live Photo extraction (still, MP4, or GIF).
